# References Directory | 参考资料目录

## English

### Purpose
This directory stores reference materials for your video script projects. You can organize:
- Research and competitor analysis
- Personal style guides and writing templates
- Custom workflow templates
- Temporary files and draft ideas
- Any materials to inspire and support your video creation

The directory structure will be automatically created based on your language preference during initialization:

### Usage Tips

#### Core Directories (Auto-created)
1. **Videos/视频**: Store analysis of reference videos - what works, what doesn't, and why
2. **Accounts/账号**: Track competitor channels - their content strategy, audience, and performance
3. **Research/调研**: General research materials, market reports, industry insights

#### Optional Directories (Create as needed)
4. **Templates/模板**: Your personal script templates, hook formulas, opening patterns
   - Save your proven frameworks for reuse
   - Customize structures that work for your niche
5. **Styles/风格**: Personal writing style guides
   - Define your brand voice and tone
   - Set guidelines for consistency across videos
6. **Temp/临时**: Temporary files, quick notes, draft ideas
   - Brain dumps and unstructured thoughts
   - Files you're not sure where to organize yet

### How to Add References
- Manually add files to appropriate subdirectories
- Create additional subdirectories based on your needs
- Reference materials can be accessed during Stage 3 (Content Research)
- Use Markdown format for easy reading and editing
- The AI agent can read files from this directory when you mention them

### 使用建议

#### 核心目录（自动创建）
1. **视频/Videos**: 存放参考视频的分析 - 什么有效、什么无效、为什么
2. **账号/Accounts**: 追踪竞品频道 - 内容策略、受众、表现数据
3. **调研/Research**: 通用调研资料、市场报告、行业洞察

#### 可选目录（按需创建）
4. **模板/Templates**: 你的个人脚本模板、钩子公式、开场套路
   - 保存你验证过有效的框架以便复用
   - 自定义适合你领域的结构
5. **风格/Styles**: 个人写作风格指南
   - 定义你的品牌语调和表达方式
   - 设置准则保持视频间的一致性
6. **临时/Temp**: 临时文件、快速笔记、草稿想法
   - 头脑风暴和未结构化的思考
   - 暂时不确定如何归类的文件

### 如何添加参考资料
- 手动添加文件到对应子目录
- 根据需要创建额外的子目录
- 参考资料可在阶段3（内容调研）时被读取使用
- 使用 Markdown 格式便于阅读和编辑
- 当你提到这些文件时，AI 助手可以读取它们
